Introducing HelloRelaxation - San Francisco corporate housing

When we created San Francisco corporate housing we had both you and your employees in mind. Your employees would like to enjoy top location, amazing furnished apartments and cutting edge community amenities. You would like your employees to enjoy all of these at affordable price for you and walking distance to your office.

Location:

Just one block from Ferry Building/Farmer's Market, held on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays, is a great place to pick up organic products from local farmers, as well as flowers and regional favorites such as artisanal breads and cheeses. On summer Thursdays, there is also free live music.

A world of city experiences is just outside the front door, from the landscaped beauty of the waterfront to the historic aura of Jackson Square. Also, a pedestrian bridge provides a convenient connection to the Embarcadero Center.

Furnished apartments:

All of our apartments are fully furnished and ready to move in.

Bedroom includes a queen-sized bed, four deluxe pillows, down blanket, lush duvets and high thread-count quality sheets.

Our gourmet kitchens are spacious, fully equipped with all major appliances and a storage for your convenience.

Living room area feature a comfortable sitting area, T.V set with D.V.D and a Wi-Fi network that runs through the entire apartment.
The dining area which is adjacent to the living room, accommodates up to four people.

Community amenities

As residents of the Gateway your employees will enjoy:

  • Helpful doorpersons in each lobby
  • Controlled-access garage parking (if needed)
  • Private balconies or terraces
  • Laundry facilities
  • Landscaped courtyards and plazas
  • Furnished roof decks with amazing views
  • Preferred membership at the Golden Gateway Tennis & Swim Club
